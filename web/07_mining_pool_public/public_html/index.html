Pool info, rules & support

Rules & payouts

  • PPLNS (Pay Per Last N Shares) — rewards follow recent contributed work
  • No account, email, or registration — your Grin address is your identity
  • Per-miner variable difficulty (vardiff) tunes itself to your hardware
  • Payouts are sent automatically over Tor to your Grin address; if your wallet is unreachable, retried every 6h for up to 7 days
  • Block rewards mature after 1,440 confirmations (~24h) before they are credited
  • Orphaned blocks are detected automatically and their rewards reversed fairly

Mining software & support

Any Cuckatoo32 miner works — GPU (lolMiner, GMiner, SRBMiner-MULTI) or ASIC (iPollo G1 / G1-Mini). Point it at your nearest region's host:port above, using grin_address.worker_name as the username (password ignored).
