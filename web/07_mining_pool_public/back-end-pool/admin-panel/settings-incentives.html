Prizes, lottery, streaks and bonuses

Incentives

All features below are off unless this is on. Prizes pay out of the prize pool — fund it before enabling payouts.

Prize Pool Funding

% of the collected pool fee diverted to the prize pool.
Miners donate N% of their payout by naming a worker donate10 (10%) or rig01-donate10. N is clamped 0–100; donate0 opts out.
Shown on the fortune board so anyone can donate. Reflect received donations into the prize pool with a manual top-up above.
Prize pool balance: … GRIN
Accounting only — the GRIN must already be in the pool wallet.

Award Prize / Bonus to an Address

Credit a contest or incentive prize straight to a miner's Grin address — no account needed. The prize lands on their pool balance and pays out to that address via the normal Tor withdrawal flow.

Join Bonus

Paid once per address, only after its first successful withdrawal (anti-Sybil gate).

Block-Finder Jackpot

Credited when the block matures; clawed back automatically if the block is orphaned.

Loyalty Streak Multiplier

Top-up funded from the prize pool — never reduces other miners' base PPLNS payout.

Lottery

Tickets ∝ valid shares (favours contribution).
One entry per qualifying address (favours small miners).

Special-occasion draws

Extra draws on fixed dates (e.g. Christmas, New Year, Grin Genesis Day = 01-15). Date is MM-DD, UTC.

Grin Transporter — payout rail #3 (coming soon)

Reserved for the self-hosted store-and-forward slate relay (Script 056) — async payouts for miners who aren't online when we pay. Disabled until it ships. The pool stays register-free: your Grin address remains your identity across every payout rail.
