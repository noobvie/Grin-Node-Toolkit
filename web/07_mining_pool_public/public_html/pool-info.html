Pool Information

Getting Started

No registration needed — your Grin address is your account. Point your miner at the pool with grin_address.worker_name as the username and rewards accrue to that address automatically.

  • Get a Grin wallet address (grin1…)
  • Configure your miner — see Connect & Start Mining for ready-made commands
  • Use grin_address.worker_name as the stratum username (any password)
  • Track earnings on My Account — payouts arrive via Tor automatically

Pool Connection Details

Stratum Server: configured by the pool operator

Mining Algorithm: Cuckatoo32

Network: mainnet

Username Format: grin_address.worker_name

Fees & Payouts

  • Pool Fee: — (PPLNS reward distribution)
  • Minimum Payout: —
  • Payouts are sent automatically over Tor to your Grin wallet address
  • If your wallet is unreachable, the payout retries every 6 hours for up to 7 days
  • Block rewards mature after 1,440 confirmations (~24h) before they are credited

Mining Rules & Policies

  • PPLNS (Pay Per Last N Shares) — rewards follow recent contributed work
  • No account, email, or registration required — address-as-identity
  • ASIC mining (Cuckatoo32) — e.g. iPollo G1/G1-Mini
  • Per-miner variable difficulty (vardiff) tunes itself to your hardware
  • Orphaned blocks are detected automatically and their rewards reversed fairly

Supported Mining Software

  • lolMiner
  • GMiner
  • SRBMiner-MULTI
  • ASIC built-in stratum clients (iPollo and compatible)

Ready-made command lines for each are on the Connect page.
