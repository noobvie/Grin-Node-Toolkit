Click a nickname for its workers. Owed = its share of every matured coinbase (split by work done, per nickname) minus what the operator has already paid out-of-band — it drops to zero once settled. The period table shows work share within a rolling window. No Grin address is stored or shown.

Payouts are settled manually. The operator sends GRIN by hand to each miner out-of-band (e.g. friends mining to this node) and records it here — this page is bookkeeping only and moves no funds.
