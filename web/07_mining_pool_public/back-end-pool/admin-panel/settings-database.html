Retention & Cleanup

Prunes raw shares, old hashrate history, and resolved alerts on a schedule. Shares are only ever deleted below a floor of confirm depth + PPLNS window (60) + safety margin, and never for a block still maturing — so PPLNS payouts and orphan reversal are never affected.
