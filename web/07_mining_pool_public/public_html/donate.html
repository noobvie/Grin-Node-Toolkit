Option 1 · for miners

Donate a slice of your payouts

No account, no extra step. Just name one of your workers donate<N> — where N is the percentage of your payouts (0–100) you want to give to the prize pool.

Examples (the donate tag can be the whole worker name or a -/_ suffix):

Worker nameResult
grin1…address.donate10Donate 10% of your payouts
grin1…address.rig01-donate5Worker “rig01”, donate 5%
grin1…address.donate00% — turn a donation back off
grin1…address.rig01No donation (normal worker)
donate101, donate-1, donatexxIgnored — treated as a normal worker name, donates nothing

Only donate0–donate100 donate. Anything out of range or mistyped is safely treated as an ordinary worker name — a typo can never donate more than you meant. The donation is applied to each payout as it's calculated.

Option 2 · for anyone

Donate directly by Slatepack

Not mining, or want to give a one-off gift? Send any amount to the pool's donation address. The operator adds received donations to the prize pool.

Where your donation goes

  • 🎁 Join bonus — a one-time welcome for new miners who reach their first payout.
  • 💥 Block-finder jackpot — a flat bonus to whoever solves a block (small miners included).
  • 🔥 Loyalty streak — a small bonus for mining consistently, day after day.
  • 🎉 Lottery — weekly and special-occasion draws, with an equal-chance pot so small miners can win too.
